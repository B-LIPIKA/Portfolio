AgriFlow

IoT Based Smart Irrigation System

AgriFlow is an innovative smart irrigation system designed to automate and optimize water usage in agriculture. Using sensors and real-time data, it enables farmers to control irrigation through a mobile app.

It reduces water wastage, ensures healthy crops, and promotes sustainable farming practices.
